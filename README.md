**Live Site:** [byrdsong-portfolio.github.io](https://byrdsong-portfolio.github.io)

## Overview

This repository contains the source code for Isaiah Byrdsong's personal portfolio website. The site serves as a centralized, publicly accessible platform for presenting Isaiah's professional background, academic achievements, research output, and ongoing development in the fields of security, intelligence, and cybersecurity.

---

## About

Isaiah Byrdsong is a Security and Intelligence professional currently pursuing a Master of Security Studies in Intelligence and Analysis alongside a Graduate Cybersecurity Certificate at Angelo State University. His work spans physical-digital security convergence, identity and access management, and critical infrastructure protection, with hands-on experience securing large-scale manufacturing facilities and corporate workforces.

As an ISC2 Candidate and ASU Distinguished Scholar, Isaiah developed this site to maintain a transparent and professional record of his credentials, research, and experience for prospective employers, academic collaborators, and professional contacts.
