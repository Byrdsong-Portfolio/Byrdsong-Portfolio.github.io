Data Sources & Methodology
[1] U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. (2024). Occupational Employment and Wage Statistics: Software Developers, Quality Assurance Analysts, and Testers (SOC 15-1252). U.S. Dept. of Labor. bls.gov/oes — Median annual wage converted to per-week; employer benefits load factor ~1.30× applied to derive total compensation.
[2] Glassdoor Economic Research. (2026). Software Engineer Salary Report, Q1 2026. Glassdoor, Inc. glassdoor.com/research — Base salary + bonus + equity for entry-to-mid-level roles annualized to per-week basis.
[3] Levels.fyi. (2026). 2025 End-of-Year Software Engineer Compensation Report. Levels.fyi LLC. levels.fyi — Total compensation crowd-sourced from verified offer letters; L3–L4 equivalent (entry-level) band applied to match BLS classification.
[4] Anthropic, Inc. (2025–2026). Claude API Pricing — claude-3 and claude-4 model families. anthropic.com/pricing — Per-million-token pricing modeled at ~40M input + ~10M output tokens per autonomous coding-agent week.
[5] OpenAI. (2024–2026). API Pricing: GPT-4o, o1, o3 series. openai.com/api/pricing — Token rates used for cross-model cost triangulation; blended effective rate applied across multi-step agentic task simulations.
[6] Eloundou, T., Manning, S., Mishkin, P., & Rock, D. (2023). GPTs are GPTs: An early look at the labor market impact potential of large language models. Science, 384(6698). doi:10.1126/science.adj0998 — Peer-reviewed; task-substitution rate estimates inform crossover timing model.
[7] McKinsey Global Institute. (2023). The economic potential of generative AI: The next productivity frontier. McKinsey & Company. mckinsey.com/mgi — Developer-role automation exposure rates inform post-2025 AI cost projection slope.
[8] Brynjolfsson, E., Li, D., & Raymond, L. R. (2023). Generative AI at work. NBER Working Paper No. 31161. National Bureau of Economic Research. nber.org/papers/w31161 — Peer-reviewed; productivity impact data contextualizes cost-per-task trajectory in AI agent workflows.
* 2027 values are model projections. AI token cost trajectory extrapolated from observed –15% to –30%/yr price reduction (2023–2025, per [4][5]), offset against +40–60%/yr growth in autonomous agent context-window consumption. Crossover Zone (~Q3 2026) marks estimated parity between weekly AI agent operational cost and median entry-level engineer weekly total compensation. Projections carry ±20% confidence interval and do not constitute investment, hiring, or procurement advice.
