## Overview

The **Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate** is an 8-course program developed by Google and offered on Coursera, designed to build job-ready skills for entry-level cybersecurity roles. The curriculum spans security foundations, risk management, network defense, Linux, SQL, and hands-on experience with SIEM and IDS tooling.
