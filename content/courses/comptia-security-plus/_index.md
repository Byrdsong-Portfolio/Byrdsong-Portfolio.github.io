## Overview

**CompTIA Security+** is the industry-standard, vendor-neutral cybersecurity certification trusted by employers worldwide. It validates the baseline skills needed to perform core security functions and is required by the U.S. Department of Defense under DoD Directive 8570.

The **SY0-701** exam focuses on the latest threats, automation, zero trust, IaC, and cloud/hybrid environments — reflecting the real-world demands of today's security roles.
