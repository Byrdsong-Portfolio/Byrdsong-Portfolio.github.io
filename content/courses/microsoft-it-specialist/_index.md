## Overview

The **Microsoft IT Specialist** certification is a globally recognized credential validating foundational and intermediate IT skills. The program covers core domains essential to modern IT roles including networking, security, cloud infrastructure, and systems administration — aligning with industry standards and real-world enterprise environments.

## Why This Certification

> [!TIP]
> Microsoft IT Specialist credentials are recognized by employers in IT support, systems administration, and cybersecurity roles. Combined with hands-on IAM experience, this certification strengthens a technical foundation for security-focused IT careers.
